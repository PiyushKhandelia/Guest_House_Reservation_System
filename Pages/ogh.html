Old Guest House (OGH) at BIT Mesra:

The Old Guest House at BIT Mesra seamlessly blends colonial and indigenous design elements, offering guests a nostalgic yet comfortable retreat. Each room exudes timeless elegance with antique furnishings, while attentive staff uphold the principle of 'Atithi Devo Bhava' to ensure a memorable stay. Culinary offerings feature a fusion of traditional and modern flavors. Surrounded by lush gardens, ponds, and tranquil lakesides, OGH provides a serene escape for introspection and relaxation. It embodies the institute's rich history and culture, inviting guests on a journey of discovery and nostalgia.
