International Guest House (IGH) at BIT Mesra:

The International Guest House (IGH) at BIT Mesra embodies the institution's commitment to global engagement. With modern amenities and traditional aesthetics, it offers spacious, well-equipped rooms and versatile facilities for conferences and meetings. Warm hospitality and culinary delights enhance the guest experience, while serene surroundings encourage relaxation and exploration. IGH serves as a hub for cross-cultural exchange and collaboration, reflecting BIT Mesra's dedication to excellence and welcoming visitors from around the world.
